About

I'm basically a classic nerd. I've been programming since I was 12, and I've been a professional programmer since I was 20. And after a long day of coding, the first thing I always did when I got home was... sit down at my computer again! I'll admit that after 25 solid years of this I got a little burned out, so I took some time off to investigate a couple of alternate career paths. But none of them really caught my fancy the way coding always has, so I'm back to my first love again.

Non-Programming Stuff

My other interests include cognitive science and its intersection with computer science. If you want to hear me talk your ear off for a couple of hours, ask me about the difference between consciousness and intelligence.

I'm also a big fan of video games, science fiction (told you I was a nerd!), and football. And, far too late in life, I finally acquired a love of cooking. If you ever do a video call with me you'll see that an avalanche of gadgets from my tiny little kitchen has overflowed into my bedroom!

Portfolio

My portfolio currently consists of one project, TrackEats.


TrackEats is a full-stack web application that allows users to accurately track the nutritional content of their diet. It's deployed in Docker containers on an AWS EC2 instance. The front end is built with TypeScript, React, Tanstack Table and Axios. The back end is built with Python, Flask, and SQLAlchemy, and an alternate pluggable back end built with Java, Spring and Hibernate is on the way. The database is MySQL, and it is secured with JWT tokens. More information can be found in the app's About page, which is linked below.
